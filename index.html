The highest quality products at the best prices

Parts World stands for reliability, derived from our commitment to supplying our customers with the highest quality products at the best prices. Since our establishment in 2002, we have grown to become a leading provider of aftermarket parts in Fiji. With our head office in Suva and branches in Ba, Tavua, Nadi, Labasa and Taveuni, we serve customers around the country. Our goal is to provide not only quality products and customer service, but the expertise and after support to fulfill all our customers’ needs.
